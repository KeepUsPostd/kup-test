Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about KeepUsPostd

Sign up for a free Starter plan, complete your brand profile, set up your first campaign, and start connecting with influencers. You can upgrade at any time as your needs grow.
We offer four plans: Starter (free forever), Growth, Pro, and Agency. Each tier unlocks more brand slots, campaigns, and support features. Visit the Pricing page to compare plans.
You set the reward type and amount per brand. When an influencer submits content and you approve it, they're rewarded automatically from your account balance. Reward types include cash, gift cards, store credit, and free products.
Yes. No contracts, no lock-in. Cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties. Your account will remain active until the end of your current billing period.
Navigate to Campaigns from the top menu, click 'Create Campaign', and follow the guided setup. You'll define the campaign goal, content requirements, reward structure, and target influencer criteria.
Market Code is your brand's unique QR code. Influencers and customers can scan it to discover your brand on KeepUsPostd. It also powers in-store purchase points when stored in a customer's app.
Purchase points reward customers for buying from your brand. In-store, the clerk scans the customer's KeepUsPostd app at checkout. Online, points are tracked automatically through connected e-commerce platforms like Shopify or WooCommerce.
Always. Influencers download and use the KeepUsPostd app for free — forever. They earn rewards from brand approvals and purchase points, with no fees or subscriptions.
Go to Manage Brands from your profile menu and click 'Add Your Brand'. Your plan determines how many brand slots are available. Each brand has its own profile, campaigns, and reward settings.
Scroll down to the Contact Support section below, or use the chat widget in the bottom-right corner. Response times depend on your plan: Starter gets AI chat, Growth gets 24-hour email support, and Pro/Agency get priority support.
