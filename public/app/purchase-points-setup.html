How It Works
1Customer opens the KeepUsPostd app and pulls up your brand's Market Code from their profile
2Store clerk scans the code from the customer's phone screen at checkout
3The purchase total is entered and linked to the customer's account
4Points are automatically awarded based on the highest tier the customer's purchase reaches — no daily cap, every qualifying transaction counts

Note: If a returning customer who already has the KeepUsPostd app scans your printed Market Code, they'll be taken directly to your brand's Learn More page — no re-download required.
