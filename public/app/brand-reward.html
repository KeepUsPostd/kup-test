Brands offering points + free product see 2x more creator applications than single-reward campaigns.

Be Specific

Clear descriptions attract 40% more creators. Say exactly what they'll receive and how they earn it.

Set Smart Limits

Adding a budget cap prevents overspending while keeping your reward active until you're ready to pause.
