Strong Titles Convert

Use action words like "Review", "Unbox", "Try". Include your brand name for recognition.

Category Matters

Creators filter by category in the Discover feed. Pick the one that fits best for maximum visibility.

Duration Sweet Spot

7–14 day campaigns get 3x more submissions than 3-day ones. Give creators time to find you.

Cash is King

$3–$5 per review is the platform sweet spot. Higher rewards = more submissions.

Mix Reward Types

Combine cash + product for 2x creator engagement. Creators love getting the product AND being paid.

Be Specific

  • Content guidelines that mention "show the product in use" get better results
  • Specify minimum video length (30s recommended)
  • Mention if you want honest vs. positive reviews
