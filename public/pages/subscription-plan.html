Can I start for free?

Yes. The Ambassador Plan is free forever. No credit card required to start.

How do influencer rewards work?

You choose the reward type — cash, free products, or discounts. Cash amounts are based on influencer tier. Rewards are funded from your account balance and only triggered when you approve content.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts. No lock-in. Cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties.

Is the influencer app always free?

Always. Influencers download and use the app for free — forever. They earn from brand approvals.
