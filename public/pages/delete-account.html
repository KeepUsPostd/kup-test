Delete Your Account

How to permanently delete your KeepUsPostd account and all associated data.

Option 1 — In App

Delete from the App

You can request account deletion directly inside the KeepUsPostd app:

  1. Open the KeepUsPostd app
  2. Tap your Profile icon (bottom right)
  3. Tap the Settings gear icon (top right)
  4. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent. Your profile, content, partnerships, wallet balance, and all associated data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

Option 2 — Email Request

Request Deletion by Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing our support team. Include the email address associated with your account and we will process your request within 7 business days.

Email support@keepuspostd.com

What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your profile (name, handle, avatar, bio)
  • All content submissions and reviews
  • Brand partnerships and campaign history
  • Wallet balance and transaction history
  • Saved content and notification history
  • Social account connections
  • Login credentials and Firebase authentication record

Some anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for platform analytics and legal compliance purposes as described in our Privacy Policy.
