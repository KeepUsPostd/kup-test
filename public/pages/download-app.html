How It Works

Four steps between you
and your next payday.

No applications. No agencies. Just your phone, your honest voice, and a brand that wants to hear it.

Step 01

Download and set up your profile

Free on App Store and Google Play. Create your influencer profile in under 3 minutes. No follower count required. No application needed. You're in.

Step 02

Browse brands in your city and niche

Discover local and national brands actively looking for authentic reviews from real customers. Filter by category, reward type or distance. Pick what fits your life.

Step 03

Record your honest take. For real.

Film your experience right from the app. No ring lights. No script. Just your raw, honest take — exactly what brands are paying for.

Step 04

Get approved. Get paid. Repeat.

The brand reviews your submission. When they approve it, your reward hits your account in cash, gift card or brand perks. No chasing. No invoicing.
