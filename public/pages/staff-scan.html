Quick Guide — Staff Scanner

  1. 🔐
    Enter the Staff PIN
    Your brand owner set a 4-digit PIN. Type it on the keypad — the screen unlocks automatically once it matches. Ask your manager if you don't have the PIN.
  2. 📱
    Identify the Customer
    Ask the customer to open their KeepUsPostd app and show their QR code. Point the camera at it — it scans automatically. No QR? Tap the Email or @ Handle tab and type their info manually.
  3. 💰
    Enter the Purchase Total
    Type in the total amount the customer spent using the number pad. Tap Award Points to confirm.
  4. ✅
    Done — Points Hit Instantly
    The confirmation screen shows how many points were awarded and the customer's new balance. Tap Next Customer to start over for the next transaction.
💡 Good to Know
• No KUP account? Tap Skip — Guest. The transaction is still recorded so the brand can see it. Tell the customer they can download KeepUsPostd and partner with the brand to start earning on future purchases.
• Below minimum spend? If the total is too low to earn points, the result screen will show $0 and the minimum required. No points are deducted — just let the customer know.
• Locking the app: Tap 🔒 Lock anytime to clear your session. The PIN is never saved — you'll need to re-enter it next time. Do this between shifts or if you hand your phone to someone else.
• Add to home screen: Tap the share button in your browser → Add to Home Screen — the scanner opens like a regular app with one tap.
