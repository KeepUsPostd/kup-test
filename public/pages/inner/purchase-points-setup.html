Tracking Script

Add this script to your store's checkout confirmation / thank-you page, just before the closing </body> tag:

<script src="https://sdk.keepuspostd.com/purchase-tracker.js"
  data-brand="keepuspostd"
  data-key="kup_live_abc123xyz"></script>

After installing, complete a test purchase on your store to verify the connection. Points will begin tracking automatically.
